# Resume A (example)

Placeholder resume A. Replace this file's content with your real resume
text (markdown is fine — bullets, headings, prose; the matcher sees the
raw text). Then point `RESUME_A_PATH` in your `.env` at the file path.

Do not commit your real resume to the repo. The `.gitignore` blocks
every file in `resumes/` except `example_*.md`.

## Sections you probably want

- Current role and target roles
- Key skills (languages, frameworks, tools)
- Years of experience
- Notable projects with measurable outcomes
- Education and certifications (optional)
- Locations and remote-OK flag
